Welcome to my data analytics portfolio! I'm Chris Curtis, a passionate data enthusiast with a strong foundation in public health and a passion for leveraging data to drive positive change. With my education in public health and my experiences in the healthcare industry, I bring a unique perspective to data analytics, combining a keen attention to detail with my passion for improving health outcomes and systems in our communities.

While earning my bachelor's degree in Public Health, I learned about disease prevention, intervention implementation, and analyzing health and survey data to develop health improvement programs. During my senior year, I worked with the Utah County Health Department on creating a program addressing mental health concerns for a Provo neighborhood; I also interned with the Utah Office of Healthcare Statistics, where I analyzed data in Excel to help address a legislative request during the 2022 Utah Legislative session and created visualizations that were presented in the April 2022 Utah Disabilities Advisory Committee meeting. I also had the opportunity to enroll in data analytics courses specifically for public health data. I learned valuable skills such as coding in SAS, analyzing Excel data, and managing nonprofits' budgets.

In my professional career, I have had the opportunity to work for both healthcare data companies and a nonprofit consulting company. At Cotiviti and Collective Health, I utilized tools such as Excel and SQL to analyze medical records and member data, clean and filter incorrect data, and use data to provide answers and drive productivity. I provided freelance services to A Village For Good during my free time. These services included analyzing public health data and resources for nonprofit reporting. I also assisted in analyzing and summarizing census and community data for a New York county's 2023 Community Needs Assessment.

In this portfolio, you'll find projects that illustrate my proficiency in extracting insights from complex datasets. These projects explain my interest in solving real-world challenges through data-driven solutions. I am committed to adding more projects in the future that illustrate my skills in data analytics and my ability to communicate with diverse groups and stakeholders.

I am thrilled to share my journey with you and demonstrate how my unique blend of expertise in public health and data analytics can contribute to your organization's success. With a deep-rooted passion for improving health outcomes and a knack for translating data into actionable insights, I can empower your team to make informed decisions that drive positive change.
